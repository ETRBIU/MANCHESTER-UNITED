CLUB HISTORY

VISION

Our goal: To be the best football club in the world, both on and off the pitch.

MISSION

Our mission is to bridge the gap between the past and present, ensuring that young fans have the same cherished experience as their parents did.

ABOUT US

Through our 141 years of heritage, we have won 66 trophies, including a record of 20 English league titles, and built a global community of 1.1 billion fans.

The club was formed as Newton Heath LYR football club in 1878. In 1892, the club split and remained under private ownership for over 100 years, eventually changing its name to Manchester United after being saved from bankruptcy in 1902.

CLUB OWNERSHIP

Currently, Manchester United owns 72.3%, and Trawlers Ltd. owns 27.7%.
